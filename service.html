SERVICES

What we offer


C4CODIN

C4CODIN is an Activity and Project based Computer Science club. The main aim of the club is to integrate the 21st century STEAM (Science,Technology,Engineering, Arts and Mathematics) skills that your kids needs at a very early stage in life.

PROGRAMMING CLASS

We offer in school training for pupils from age 4 years upwards on Computer programming, Website designs, Microsoft office packages, Mobile Application, 2d and 3d programming, Game design , physical computation using Python and raspberry pi and so much more..

WEEKEND C4CODIN

We offer personalised weekend services for kids and adult on computer programming, website designs, Microsoft office packages, robotics and so much more..





SUMMER BOOTCAMP

We organise IN SCHOOL summer programme as a bootcamp to introduce programming and STEAM {SCIENCE,TECHNOLOGY,ENGINEERING,ARTS AND MATHEMATICS}.

CERTIFIED

We assist in geting certified in programming and Microsoft Office Packages

EDUCATIONAL CONSULTANCY

We believe if we teach today as we were taught yesterday, we are robbing our kids’ tomorrow. Hence, We believes incorporated 21st century skills through experiments and demonstrations to increasing the knowledge of Computer science and “algorithmic thinking” in our digital world.
