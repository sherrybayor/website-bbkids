ABOUT US


BBRIGHT KIDS is an educational consultancy organization that believes incorporated 21st century skills through experiments and demonstrations to increasing the knowledge of Computer science and “algorithmic thinking” in our digital world.To achieve this have a club called C4CODIN .
C4CODIN is an Activity and Project based computer science class. The main aim of the club is to integrate the 21st century skills that your kids need at a very early stage in life.


The earlier we introduce children to programming, the more comfortable they will be when presented with more in-depth learning opportunities in secondary school. Also, early exposure to programming, helps teach children how important it is to understand computers as the valuable tools they are rather than merely fun playthings.
While it’s important to prepare kids for the modern workplace, computer science is about much more than getting a job in technology sector. Research shows that computer science helps students improve in core areas like math, logic, and even reading comprehension. Often people think of programming or coding as computer science but that is just one element. At its core, computer science is the study of how to use logical thinking to identify, simplify and solve complex problems.


Our C4CODIN can be done two ways
1. Unplugged in this package most courses are done offline with worksheet and craft to teach each concept. Little online practice is required.
2. Plugged in this package most courses are online and worksheets are also available to help relate the concept to real life situations. Home play and assessment are all done online.
