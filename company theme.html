About Us


BBRIGHT KIDS is an educational consultancy organization that believes incorporated 21st century skills through experiments and demonstrations to increasing the knowledge of Computer science and “algorithmic thinking” in our digital world.


Computer Science boils down to solving problems with very specific sets of instructions because computers only do exactly what they are told to do. Learning to think like a computer scientist or programmer helps children break down problems, think in logical sequences, and use precise language to give instructions. Computer Science is fun, collaborative, and creative! It’s designed to inspire students to continue learning how technology improves real world relationships, connections, and life.


Our Values


MISSION: We aspire to incorporate 21st century skills into kids at early stage in life. 21st century skill like Software development, Algorithm thinking, Artificial intelligence, Virtual and Augumented Reality e.t.c and to make the technology producers


VISION: We believe that students learn best when they are intrinsically motivated. We prioritize learning experiences that are active, relevant to students’ lives, and provide students authentic choice. We encourage students to be curious, solve personally relevant problems and to express themselves through creation.
